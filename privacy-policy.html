Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

This policy explains how OculaPrime handles data when you use our TikTok integration.

1. Data Collection via TikTok API

We access your TikTok public profile and content posting permissions via OAuth. This data is used exclusively to facilitate video uploads as requested by the user.

2. Data Storage

OculaPrime does not store your TikTok login credentials or personal profile data on our servers. All authentication is handled by TikTok.

3. Data Deletion

Users may revoke our access at any time through their TikTok App settings. Since we do not store persistent user data, revoking access removes all connectivity.
